Projects

Tools Used:

Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Pencil & Paper

My Role & Responsibilities

As the former Lead Designer for Scentuals, my role revolved heavily on marketing and design. I created labels for new products, revamped existing labels to suit their new branding and style, designed flyers and catalogues, as well as created promotional emails to distibutors such as Pharmasave and Save On Foods.

Major Contributions

  • Brand creator for the new spa and retail line Organic Originals
  • Redesigned the entire Baby Collection as well as illustrated the characters that are printed on the labels and tubes
  • Assisted the Web Developer with the overall front-end look of the new website that's soon to be launched

What I've Learnt

I learned to work in a fast-pace environment while being assertive in getting projects done on time. Also, I would constantly be in contact with factories in Vietnam and China in printing new labels. I learned to be very specific and that any pictures or direct links to files is always very helpful. It reduces the amount of confusion and there would be less waiting for a response for more clarification.
