Project Description

For 3 weeks in a group of 2, we were tasked to design a company website of our choice with the following requirements:

  • company updates/events
  • about the company
  • how to contact the business
  • a multi-product listing
  • one product detailed view
  • show an entire purchasing process
  • appropriate means for users to give feedback

Process

I implemented all the interactions that users would face when they navigate through the log-in, contact us, and add to cart. I was also responsible for creating the navigation through log-in, contact us, and add to cart. I also created the pages of About, Blog, and Checkout.

Due to the fact that I am not as strong of a coder compared to my partner, I designed the visual aesthics and the experience when the user clicked on to a detailed view of a particular sunglasses. I first sketched out the view, then tried coding that specific part myself by testing out rotations with CSS but at the end, the rotated shape needed to be designed as an SVG or the modal view would not work.

Outcome

In only a few weeks, we were able to create a functioning responsive website that visually pleasing and has a unique feature of viewing and commenting on products. The website can be viewed through desktops, tablets, and phones.
