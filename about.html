A little about myself...

My name is Levona and I’m a third year student studying Interactive Arts & Technology (IAT) at SFU with a two year chemistry background. My passion lies in experience design and graphic design.

I love to explore and experiment by doing hands-on work. I believe design is a way of thinking as well as communicating an idea to a broader audience. Design encourages new opportunities, allowing for new ideas to emerge or existing ones to be fixed.

On the side, I volunteer as an Artworks & Branding Coordinator for a non-profit organization called Sawa World where I collaborate with the Directors in creating promotional materials for events as well as create illustrations of solution materials. As a hobby, I also create custom cards for peers and coworkers.
